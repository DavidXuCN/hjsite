About Huijia I & E Co.

Our company established in 2004 and located in Taizhou city,Zhejiang province,China. We are specialized in international trade for many years. Now our products are exported all over the world.

Our main products included three categories: Plastic necessities, Car accessories , Plastic extrusion machine, etc..

Our company respected "practical, hard work, responsibility" spirit of enterprise. Through years of practice, now we own a completely mature management model, efficient quality control system. We believe that our years of experience will provide you with a perfect business experience.
